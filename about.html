About PGCore

A Safe, Secure & Comfortable Home for Every Girl

Who We Are

PGCore is a smart and user-friendly Girls PG Management System designed to simplify the way paying guest accommodations are managed and experienced. Our platform connects residents and administrators on a single digital dashboard, making daily operations smooth, transparent, and efficient.

We aim to provide a safe, organized, and reliable environment for girls seeking comfortable PG living. Our mission is to enhance the PG experience through smart solutions, clarity, and convenience.
