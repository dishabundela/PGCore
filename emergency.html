Safety Guidelines

  • Stay calm and do not panic during emergencies.
  • Immediately contact the PG warden or emergency services.
  • In case of fire, use stairs instead of elevators.
  • Keep emergency numbers saved in your phone.
  • Do not ignore suspicious activities; report immediately.
  • Keep first aid kit in your room.
  • Always lock your room while leaving.
  • Follow evacuation plan if instructed.

Basic First Aid Tips

  • For minor cuts, clean wound and apply antiseptic.
  • For burns, cool with running water immediately.
  • For fainting, lay person flat and elevate legs.
  • In case of serious injury, call ambulance immediately.
  • Do not give food/water to unconscious person.
