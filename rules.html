PG Rules & Regulations

Timings

Entry allowed till 10:00 PM. Late entry requires prior permission.

Silence must be maintained after 10:30 PM to ensure a peaceful environment. Late entry is allowed only with prior permission from the management.

Cleanliness

Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas. Fine may be charged for negligence. All residents must keep their rooms and common areas clean at all times. Personal belongings should be properly arranged and waste must be disposed of responsibly. Failure to maintain cleanliness may result in fines or warnings from management.

Noise Policy

Loud music, shouting, or any form of disturbance is strictly prohibited. Silence must be maintained after 10:30 PM to ensure a peaceful environment for all residents. Repeated violations may lead to strict action by the management

Restricted Activities

All residents must follow PG rules and cooperate with management at all times. Smoking, alcohol, and illegal activities are strictly prohibited inside the premises. Any violation of rules may result in fines or cancellation of accommodation.

Electricity Usage

Residents are requested to use electricity responsibly and avoid unnecessary power consumption. High-power appliances such as heaters, irons, and induction stoves are not allowed in rooms without permission. Always switch off lights, fans, and electrical devices when not in use to ensure safety and energy saving.

Safety & Security

Residents must ensure doors are properly locked and follow all safety instructions.

Room & Property Care

PG property, furniture, and fittings must be used carefully. Damages will be chargeable

Food & Kitchen Rules

Food should be consumed in designated areas only. Wastage of food is not allowed.
