PG Rules & Guidelines


General Rules

  • All residents must maintain discipline and respect other residents.
  • Identity proof is mandatory during check-in and must be submitted to the PG owner.
  • Smoking, alcohol, and illegal activities are strictly prohibited inside the PG premises.
  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas.
  • Electricity and water should be used responsibly.

Entry & Exit Rules

  • Residents must return before the allowed entry time (usually 9:00 PM).
  • Late entry requires prior permission from the PG owner.
  • Entry after midnight is strictly restricted unless approved.

Visitor Rules

  • All visitors must be registered in the Visitor Log.
  • Visitors are allowed only in common areas and during permitted hours.
  • Visitors must carry valid ID proof.
  • Overnight stay of visitors is not allowed without permission.

Payment Rules

  • Monthly rent must be paid before the due date.
  • Late payment may result in penalty charges.
  • Security deposit is refundable after room inspection.

Food & Mess Rules

  • Food timings must be followed strictly.
  • Wastage of food is not allowed.
  • Mess complaints should be reported through the Complaint section.

Safety & Security

  • Always lock your room when leaving.
  • Do not share your room keys with others.
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately.
  • Emergency contacts are available in the Emergency section.

Damage & Maintenance

  • Any damage to PG property must be reported immediately.
  • Residents are responsible for damages caused by them.
  • Maintenance requests must be submitted via Complaint page.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Violation of rules may lead to warnings or penalties.
  • Repeated violations may result in termination of stay.
